What are the problems of governmet in Nigeria?

There are roadblocks to a strong democracy in Nigeria at all levels of government. Conflict-triggered by political competition and communal, ethnic, religious or resources allocation rivalries-poses a major of threat to democracy. Therefore Corruption pervades the daily lives of Nigerians. Good Governance bring peace and unity among people while bad governance brings conflict Obasango Quote

There are four(4) causes of bad governance

  • Unemployment
  • Political ethic
  • Religion
  • Resources allocation e.g crude oil etc
